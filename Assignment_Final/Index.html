Goal of the Game

You and the computer roll a pair of dice 3 times the player with the highest score at the end of the game wins.

How To Play

play each round by clicking the 'Start Round' button. Reset the game by clicking the 'Reset Game' button.

Scoring

Each die face is worth that many points except for one which is worth zero points.

Doubles are worth the total of the two dice times two for example if you roll two fives the score will be (5+5)*2=20
